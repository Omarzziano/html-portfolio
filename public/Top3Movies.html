My favorite movies of 2025

TOP 3 MOVIES

Sinners

I'm a sucker for epic vampire movies. I was feeling goosebumps for a good amount of the film. Godbless Ryan Coogler. Great amount of jumpscares, great visual effects, great music! I was levitating on my seat, specially at THAT one scene. Great story. Will buy and watch again.

Mickey 17

Caught them in 32k. Man I enjoyed this movie, great actors, phenomenal acting. If it's got alien-looking worms in it, it's an automatic Oscar winner for me.

Novacaine

Jack Quaid made me laugh my ass off and startle with lots of the scenes in this movie. Greaaat acting. Great effects. Absolute Cinema.
